Tags

The archive uses a hierarchical scheme to organize the over 300 tags it employs. This scheme follows the structure of the PƒÅli teachings when possible, but I have needed to invent new categories for modern subjects. Mirroring the structure of the teachings, individual tags often appear multiple times in the hierarchy. For example, Equanimity (upekkha) appears as a factor of Right Concentration and in the lists of Factors of Awakening, Divine Abidings, and Perfections. One can explore the tag hierarchy by starting with the root tags and successively expanding them to reveal the subtags they contain.
