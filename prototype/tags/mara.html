2014 Thanksgiving Monastic Retreat, Session 7 – Ajahn Pasanno – Nov. 28, 2014


7. (6:07) “A question for all the bhikkhus: Can you please recall a time when you were intensely attacked by the armies of Mara and had a lot of suffering and what did you do to overcome it? What strategy did you use or whether nothing worked at all and you just had to be patient with it and accept it? Thank you for your teaching.” [Monastic life] [Māra] [Suffering] [Patience]
