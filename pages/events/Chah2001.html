Remembering Ajahn Chah Weekend
Ajahn Sumedho and Ajahn Pasanno
Apr. 28, 2001 to Apr. 29, 2001
Wat Buddha in Portola Valley, California
3 sessions, 18 excerpts, 58:54 total duration

On April 28-29, 2001, over 200 people gathered in Portola Valley to honor the life and teachings of Ajahn Chah. Senior monastic disciples, former monks and nuns, and people who briefly encountered Ajahn Chah shared their recollections of this great teacher.
