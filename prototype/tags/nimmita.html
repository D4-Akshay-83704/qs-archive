2015 Thanksgiving Monastic Retreat, Session 4 – Ajahn Pasanno – Nov. 24, 2015


23. “It seems that nimittas can appear before the mind is fully settled in concentration. Is it useful to understand what that is happening? Should one ignore the nimitta until concentration is firmly established? Or is there some other response or skillful way to work with the nimitta while establishing samadhi?” [Nimmita]
