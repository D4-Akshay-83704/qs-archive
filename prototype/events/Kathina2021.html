Abhayagiri Kaṭhina 2021

Ajahn Pasanno, Ajahn Amaro and Ajahn Ñāniko

Abhayagiri Monastery spread the 2021 Kaṭhina festival over a period of seven days to accommodate the necessities of the pandemic. Question and answers were recorded on days two and four.
