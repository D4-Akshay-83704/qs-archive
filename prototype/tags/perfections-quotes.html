Tudong Stories at Spirit Rock, Session 3 – Ajahn Pasanno – Jun. 2, 2011


10. (5:27) Quote: “It all comes back to that simple quality of mindfulness. From the mindfulness, then the different qualities of practice that we need to rely on are cultivated.” [Tudong] [Mindfulness] [Faculties] // [Concentration] [Thai] [Translation] [Discernment] [Perfections]

Recollection: "It's rare that Ajahn Chah would use [the Pāli term] pañña on its own. More often than not, he would use satipañña, which is mindfulness and wisdom together." [Ajahn Chah] [Pāli]
