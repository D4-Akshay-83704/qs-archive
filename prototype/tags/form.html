Death and Dying, Session 2 – Ajahn Yatiko, Jeanne Daskais and Debbie Stamp – May. 9, 2014


12. (1:20) Comment: You spoke of death as the dissolution of the body and awareness coming home. The life that we think we're leading is not the life that we know about when we're aware. [Death] [Form] [Knowing itself] [Nature of mind]

Response by Ajahn Pasanno: When death comes, our life is like something that is built up out of crystal that becomes a pile of rubble in the end. It's so important to recognize that within that there is this possibility of awareness and purity of heart. [Similes]
